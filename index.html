Visit Ifon

Come and enjoy culture and sport in this vibrant town close to the river-Ose in Ondo state of Nigeria.

Top three activities to do at Ifon

Attend a show or a concert

The Ifon Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for piknic in the local park

The Ifon Park is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Get sweaty at the sports field

Play volleyball, football, basket ball, or lift some weights.
